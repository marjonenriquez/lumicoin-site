Privacy Policy

Effective Date: May 12, 2025

1. What We Collect

Health Data (via Apple HealthKit)

Types Collected: Steps, sleep duration, hydration, exercise minutes, and other metrics you explicitly permit. Purpose: To calculate your LümiCoin earnings — a behavioral health currency that rewards real‑world self‑care.

We never store raw HealthKit data on our servers. Only aggregated, anonymized summaries (e.g. total coins earned) sync to your LümiCoin profile.

Account Information (via Firebase Authentication)

Collected: Email address, password (securely hashed), and optional display name. Purpose: To let you securely log in, sync across devices, and access your rewards.

App Usage Data (anonymized)

Collected: Feature usage events, error logs (e.g., Crashlytics). Purpose: To improve app stability and user experience. We do not use this data for advertising or share it with third parties.

2. How We Protect Your Data

• All data is encrypted in transit via HTTPS.
• Firebase Authentication follows industry‑standard security protocols.
• HealthKit data remains local unless summarized for reward calculations.
• We never sell or share your data with advertisers or brokers.

3. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • View and update your account information.
  • Request deletion of your LümiCoin account and associated data.
  • Opt out of analytics collection (coming in future versions).

To exercise any right, please email us at privacy@lumicoin.app.

4. Cookies & Web Tracking

Our website (lumicoin.app) does not use cookies or third‑party trackers that collect personal data.

5. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy to reflect product or legal changes. When we do, we’ll revise the Effective Date above and, for significant updates, notify users in‑app.
